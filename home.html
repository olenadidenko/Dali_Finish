FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

FAQ
How far in advance should I schedule my move?

For optimal planning and flexibility, it's advisable to book Dali Moving services 4-6 weeks in advance. This ensures a well-coordinated and stress-free move, allowing you to secure your preferred date and time. However, we understand that circumstances may vary, and we also offer last-minute moving services to accommodate urgent relocation needs. Whether you plan ahead or require immediate assistance, Dali Moving is here to provide reliable and efficient moving solutions tailored to your timeline.

How do you calculate the cost of a move?

At Dali Moving, we calculate the cost of your move based on an hourly rate for our moving services. Additionally, there may be extra charges for specific factors such as items exceeding 200 lbs, navigating stairs, or other unique considerations. We aim for transparency in our pricing, and during the quoting process, we provide a comprehensive breakdown of all potential costs associated with your move. If you have any specific questions or would like a detailed quote tailored to your needs, don't hesitate to contact our team.

Are there any hidden costs or extra charges?

Dali Moving offers an hourly rate for our moving services, providing transparency and flexibility for our customers. Additionally, there are specific considerations that may incur additional charges:

Excess Weight: For items exceeding 200 lbs, such as big safes, slate pool tables, air hockey tables, treadmills, gym equipment etc. extra charges may apply due to the additional effort and resources required for their safe transport.

Stairs: If your apartment is located above the main floor and requires moving items via stairs instead of an elevator, there may be an additional charge to account for the added complexity involved in navigating staircases. It's important to note that this additional charge pertains specifically to apartments and does not apply to houses or condos.

Our goal is to ensure fair and clear pricing, and we provide detailed information about any potential extra charges during the quoting proce

Do you require a deposit?

For local moves expected to be completed on the same day, no deposit is required. However, for long-distance and cross-country moves, a deposit is necessary. The deposit is fully refundable in the event of a cancellation, provided you, our client, give us proper and prior notice. Feel free to communicate any changes by email or phone message. For more details, please refer to our cancellation policy.

Is there a cancellation policy?

We appreciate your understanding in the event of a cancellation. To avoid any charges or cancellation fees, we kindly request a minimum of 48 hours' notice for move cancellations. If a cancellation occurs without prior notice, a minimum moving charge of $250.00 may be applied, or any deposit may be withheld.

Do you provide moving supplies?

Yes, we offer essential equipment and supplies for free as part of our moving service, including dollies, shrink wrap, tape, straps, and moving blankets for item protection. Additionally, we provide a variety of competitively priced moving boxes. If you wish to keep the moving blankets after the move, the payment for them will be calculated separately. Rest assured, our goal is to make your move as convenient and efficient as possible.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods to cater to your convenience. You can pay with cash, credit card (Visa or MasterCard), certified check, or e-transfer. However, please note that we do not accept PayPal or personal cheques. Feel free to choose the option that suits you best.

Do I need to pay taxes?

Yes. 5% GST applies to the total amount of the move cost.

When do you start counting the moving hourly rate?

We begin counting the hourly rate from the moment we arrive at your scheduled time, until the move is successfully completed.
